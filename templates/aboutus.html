Who We Are

At MYBOOKJOURNAL, we're readers first — just like you. We created this space to give book lovers a thoughtful and personal way to track their reading journey. Whether you're diving into fiction, exploring memoirs, or revisiting childhood favorites, we’re here to make every note and every memory count.

Our Vision

We envision a world where reading isn't just tracked, but treasured. Our goal is to inspire mindful reading habits, help you reflect on the stories that shape you, and build a digital sanctuary where your literary journey can grow freely — quietly, meaningfully, and uniquely yours.
