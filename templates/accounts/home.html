Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to sign up to use the website? ▶
Signing up unlocks all features like your personal dashboard, goal tracking, wishlist, and notepad access ‚Äî helping you build your personalized reading journey.
What can I do from the dashboard after signing up? ▶
You can add, view, and delete books, write notes in your notepad, track your reading goals, and manage your wishlist ‚Äî all from one place.
How does the Notepad work and what is it for? ▶
The notepad is a free space to jot down anything ‚Äî to-do lists, thoughts, reminders, or personal ideas ‚Äî unrelated to your reading logs.
How does the progress bar function in the profile page? ▶
The progress bar shows how close you are to your reading goal. As you mark books complete, the bar shifts from red to green and displays a success message once you reach 100%.
How can I personalize my profile? ▶
By clicking the profile option in the nav bar, you can upload a profile picture, add your full name, a motto or quote, and more details.
Can I edit or update my reading goals or book entries later? ▶
Yes, you can update or delete your books, modify start or end dates, or even change your yearly goal count at any time from your dashboard for flexibility.
Is my wishlist and notepad data visible to others? ▶
No, your wishlist and notepad entries are completely private. Only you can view and manage them through your personal dashboard.
What is the Wishlist section for? ▶
The wishlist lets you dream big! Add life goals like dream places, cars, or anything important to you. You can check them off once achieved.
